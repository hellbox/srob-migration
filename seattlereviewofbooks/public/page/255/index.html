Last Friday night, at the Hugo House’s very last reading in its old site, House Executive Director Tree Swenson pulled off a first in the history of readings: her introduction was perhaps more interesting than the headlining authors. Swenson told the audience about the House’s penultimate reading, an event called “Where the House Was,” hosted by Frances McCue.
