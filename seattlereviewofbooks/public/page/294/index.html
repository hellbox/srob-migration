Drawn this way

In the beginning was the author. She wrote into a blank void, empty of human emotion, empty of human intention. There, she placed the character, who stood looking around and scratching herself occasionally. The author gave the character a desire, and the character moved. For years, film nerds and comics nerds used to get in silly fights over which medium was better.
