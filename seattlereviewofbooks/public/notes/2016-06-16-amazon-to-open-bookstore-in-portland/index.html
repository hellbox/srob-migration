Amazon to open bookstore in Portland

After Seattle and San Diego, Portland gets the “honor” of hosting the third Amazon Books brick-and-mortar store.

“We are excited to be bringing Amazon Books to Washington Square, and we are currently hiring store managers and associates. Stay tuned for additional details down the road.”

Who’s going to be the first person to showroom a book at Portland’s Amazon Books and buy it from Powell’s?
