MRA Dilbert

Did you know that Scott Adams, the cartoonist behind Dilbert, is a real dillhole? It’s true! And the Tumblr MRA Dilbert performs a real public service by publishing Scott Adams’s actual words in the word balloons of his characters. This is such a clever way to comment on Adams’ toxic beliefs. Contrasting them with the bland inoccuousness of his cartoons — although calling Adams’s drawing “cartoons” feels like an insult to cartooning — lays all the ugliness of his ideas and work out for anyone to see. ... Searching for meaning? Tickets are available now!

Seattle University’s Search for Meaning festival is an underrated event in Seattle’s literary calendar.
