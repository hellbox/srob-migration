Three Poems

disturbed bodies
(ours)
and on the other side
a moon
possibly…
watching

switching pens there
subtle hope
& your tired body
tired
breathing hard…
to exist

My minor violence
Toward the gazelle
Is unforgivable
Now making lists
Of the longing that will stay…
And the longing that
Will be fixed in images
Hatred splits us
But its necessity
Is like water
And love
