<h5 class="noindent">**Monday, October 23: An Evening with G. Willow Wilson**</h5>
<p class="noindent">Humanities Washington brings Seattle memoirist, novelist, and comics writer G. Willow Wilson to the stage for a conversation about creating the world’s most famous Muslim superhero, what it means to be a political writer, and how to juggle fame across two or three literary disciplines. </p> 

<p class="noindent">*Langston Hughes Performing Arts Institute, 104 17th Ave S. 682-1770 x102 http://humanities.org. $20. All ages. 7:30 p.m. *</p>
