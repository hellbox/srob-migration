#actualasianpoets speak out

The Asian-American Writers Workshop has published an anthology of essays (self-described as “a Forum”) titled “After Yi-Fen Chou.” In it, 19 Asian-American authors respond to the Best American Poetry 2015 controversy. Some of the pieces are hilarious. Many are furious at Michael Derrick Hudson for submitting work under the name Yi-Fen Chou.
