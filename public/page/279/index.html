Tennessee to name an official state book

I am all for the idea of states naming official books. (Make Way for Ducklings is, adorably, Massachusetts’s official children’s book.) But I’m not a fan of this move: Tennessee is poised to make history as the first state in the nation to recognize the Holy Bible as its official book.
