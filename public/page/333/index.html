Searching for meaning? Tickets are available now!

Seattle University’s Search for Meaning festival is an underrated event in Seattle’s literary calendar. This year’s Search for Meaning slate includes spiritual writer Tracy Kidder; Suki Kim, who went undercover in North Korea and lived to tell the tale; and journalist Sam Quiñones.
