And everything a scenic view

…Look, a cat killed a cardinal on an emerald lawn. For so many reasons it shouldn’t have been beautiful. But that’s also the kind of book I like best. Looking, according to Lia Purpura, is always instructive. She describes what she finds, and how it registers in her body. Feelings that are complicated, hard to pin down, uncomfortable or confusing call her to deeper inquiry. She’s refined her practice through three collections of essays (Increase, Rough Likeness, and On Looking,) and three of poetry (The Brighter the Veil, Stone Sky Lifting, and King Baby). ...
