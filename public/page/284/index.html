Jim Harrison, dead at 78

Jim Harrison, the prolific half-blind masculinist writer of many books of fiction and poetry, has died at 78. Harrison was perhaps the last of a certain kind of writer, a craggy individualist compared often (to his apparent annoyance) to Hemingway and Faulkner; a man of great appetites (of which food should be included, as he wrote extensively on the subject), an outdoorsman, and, as might be imagined, an iconoclast of the particular rugged American variety. ... Written under the influence
