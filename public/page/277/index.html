Isn't Easter already past? Not if you're in the Orthodox church

If you’re in the Orthodox church, Easter is still ahead, on May 1st this year. We’re welcoming back sponsor Charlotte Riggle, whose children’s book Catherine’s Pascha follows a little girl’s quest to stay awake for the overnight Pascha, or Easter, service. If you’re not Orthodox, maybe you’ll be wondering why the family next door are loading up the car with sleepy children all dressed up come April 30th. This book is a great way of understanding the faith of your neighbors and friends, and introducing children to the many ways that different people in the world worship. ... Read more
