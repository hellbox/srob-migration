1. Artist Trust’s Grants for Artists Projects submissions process is open. Sixty Washington State artists will receive grants of $1500 for art-related projects, which they define as “materials, equipment, and artist fees needed for the development, completion, or presentation of new work; publication; travel for creative research; documentation; and workshops for professional development.” Here’s a PDF explaining the rules. Submissions must be received by May 23rd of this year.
