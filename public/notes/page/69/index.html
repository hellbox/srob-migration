October 18, 2016

Fir

Polygon tufts kitely incurved hood the fir cones from rainfall. They respond by closing tight like the armadillo married the ent and gave birth to convex reflections of skylight bursting chrome. No slither for the deluge the fir cones may now ripen a living piece of architecture. And when the branches shed, the needles burn in captivity, in ceremony, to bless and protect continuum.
