Open Call for a Public Diversity Editor

The Seattle Review of Books is looking for a freelance Public Diversity Editor. Since that’s a term we just made up, we should probably explain what it is. We’re two white guys. One of our goals with the site was to try to do a good job with representation, to create a site that reflects the diversity of the literary community. We thought one way to guard against us succumbing to our own unconscious biases would be to hire an outside person to check our work and report back to our readers. ... Read more
