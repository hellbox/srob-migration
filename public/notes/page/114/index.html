A quick note on problems with GiveBIG

We’re always excited to promote GiveBIG every year, but this year it seems that the website is having serious issues. As in, it’s impossible to donate through the GiveBIG site right now. Many nonprofits are reporting not receiving donations in over an hour, and the Seattle Foundation’s calls for people to “refresh” the site don’t seem to be working. In short: GiveBIG this year is pretty much a clusterfuck. There will for sure be hell to pay in the coming days — this is a big day for Seattle’s nonprofit community and this will surely affect the annual income of local organizations across the board. ...
