Oh Wednesday
Full of rain
An argument
With no one

Junk mail
In the mailbox
Hummingbird
In the fall smoke tree

Waiting it out
Coffee going
Cold again
In the untouched

Cup
My father
Not my father
Gone since June

His voice a message
On my phone
Everything
Sounds like rain

In the living room
Of his greeting
