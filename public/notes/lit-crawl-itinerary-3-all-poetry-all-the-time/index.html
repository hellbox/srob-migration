Tomorrow night is Lit Crawl, a bacchanalia of literary events spread over four courses across Capitol Hill, First Hill, and downtown. [You can find the full schedule here](http://litcrawl.org/seattle/2017-schedule), but we wanted to provide a few possible itineraries for people who have a hard time making up their minds. Come back tomorrow for the next itnerary. [Here's yesterday's itinerary.](http://www.seattlereviewofbooks.com/notes/2017/10/17/lit-crawl-itinerary-2-no-white-dudes/)
