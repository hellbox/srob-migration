[Salim Ali](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Salim_Ali) was an Indian ornithologist and naturalist. His 1941 book [_The Book of Indian Birds_](https://archive.org/details/BookOfIndianBirds) went through at least fourteen editions. He won the second J. Paul Getty Wildlife Conservation prize, from the World Wildlife Fund in 1975. Ali died in 1987 at the age of 90. Today is his birthday.
