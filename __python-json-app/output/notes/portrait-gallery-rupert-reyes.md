**Monday April 24th: *Breaking the Bond* Reading**</p>

<p class="noindent">
The very fine Texas playwright Rupert Reyes brings a staged reading from his latest work-in-progress play, *Breaking the Bond*, to the U District’s own Jack Straw Gallery. Featuring local Spanish-speaking readers, this play discusses topics of deportation, anchor babies, and national identity. (Reyes has also acted in the film Office Space.) <br />*Jack Straw Gallery, 4261 Roosevelt Way N.E., 634-0919, http://jackstraw.org . Free. All ages. 7:30 p.m.*
