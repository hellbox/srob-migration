* It's not very often that a new media outlet debuts in Seattle, so let's pay attention to [*Unite Seattle*](http://uniteseattlemag.com/), which went live today. They publish coverage of arts and news in Seattle with an eye toward LGBTQ issues — and they have [a dedicated books page](http://uniteseattlemag.com/books/)! 

* The president of the United States of America is [still whining](https://www.usnews.com/news/top-news/articles/2018-01-10/stung-by-wolff-book-trump-calls-for-stronger-us-libel-laws) about how he should be able to ban a book because he doesn't like it.

<blockquote>Our current libel laws are a sham and a disgrace and do not represent American values or American fairness so we’re going to take a strong look at that... We are going to take a strong look at our country’s libel laws so that when somebody says something that is false and defamatory about someone, that person will have meaningful recourse in our courts.</blockquote>
